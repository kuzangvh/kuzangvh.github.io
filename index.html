About Me

Where clinical precision
meets data science

I'm a Healthcare Data Analyst and Data Scientist completing my MS in Data Science at Clarkson University, with a background of five years working directly with patients as a Radiation Therapist and Radiologic Technologist.

That clinical foundation shapes everything I build. I've worked at Trinity Health (MI,USA), Corewell Health (MI, USA), Beacon Health (IN,USA), and Mater Dei Hospital (BYO, Zimbabwe), developing deep expertise in CT, MRI, and radiation oncology workflows — which means I understand healthcare data from the inside out.

Today I combine that knowledge with modern data science tools to build systems that make healthcare smarter, safer, and more human. I'm also a Graduate Assistant at Sponsored Research Services at Clarkson, where I manage research databases, create communications, and conduct faculty interviews.
